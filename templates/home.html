Our Team

Arjan Buikema | Founder

Email: arjan@pendulumadvisors.com

While completing a Bsc. in Business Economics in 1993, Arjan moved to Johannesburg as an intern at ABN Amro (then Huysamer Stals), South Africa's largest Afrikaans-speaking broking firm. While there, he wrote his thesis on the different risks involved in the markets in which the company provided services. At the end of 1993, he moved permanently to South Africa to become an investment analyst at ABN Amro, focusing on Electronics and Electrical and Publishing and Printing sectors. In 1996, he joined Societe Generale South Africa, focusing on the small and mid-sized companies.
In 1999, he moved to Cape Town, having joined Old Mutual Asset Managers. While there, he researched the Media and Internet sectors. After six years at Old Mutual, Arjan joined the international team at Sanlam Investment Managers, where his focus was more broadly on consumer-related industries across the globe, particularly focused on Asia. Early 2009, he joined Naspers' Internet division in a supporting role to help the group with its M&A strategy. In 2012, he founded Pendulum Advisors, a specialist boutique focusing on supporting clients with the their investment decisions through extensive due diligence.
